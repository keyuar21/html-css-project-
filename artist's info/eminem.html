Eminem

Biography

Eminem, born Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his intricate lyrics and controversial content, Eminem is one of the most influential and best-selling artists in the history of rap music. His debut album, "Infinite," was released in 1996, but it was his 1999 album "The Slim Shady LP" that brought him to mainstream success. Since then, he has released several critically acclaimed and commercially successful albums, including "The Marshall Mathers LP," "The Eminem Show," and "Encore."

Discography Highlights

Some of Eminem's notable albums include:

  • The Slim Shady LP (1999)
  • The Marshall Mathers LP (2000)
  • The Eminem Show (2002)
  • Encore (2004)
  • Relapse (2009)
  • Recovery (2010)
  • The Marshall Mathers LP 2 (2013)
  • Kamikaze (2018)
