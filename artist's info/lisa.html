LISA

Biography

LISA, born Lalisa Manoban, is a Thai singer, rapper, and dancer based in South Korea. She is a member of the globally successful girl group BLACKPINK. LISA made her solo debut in 2021 with the single album "LALISA," which showcased her versatility as an artist and solidified her popularity as a solo performer. Known for her charismatic stage presence and powerful performances, LISA has garnered a massive following worldwide.
