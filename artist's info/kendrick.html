Kendrick Lamar

Biography

Kendrick Lamar, born Kendrick Lamar Duckworth, is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Rising to prominence with his mixtape "Section.80," Lamar has been acclaimed for his insightful and socially conscious lyrics. His 2012 album "good kid, m.A.A.d city" was a critical and commercial success, solidifying his place in modern hip-hop. Lamar's subsequent albums, including "To Pimp a Butterfly," "DAMN.," and "Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers," have earned him widespread recognition and numerous awards.

Discography Highlights

Some of Kendrick Lamar's notable albums include:

  • Section.80 (2011)
  • good kid, m.A.A.d city (2012)
  • To Pimp a Butterfly (2015)
  • DAMN. (2017)
  • Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers (2022)
